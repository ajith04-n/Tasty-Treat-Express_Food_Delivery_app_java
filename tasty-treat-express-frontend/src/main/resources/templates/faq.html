FAQ's

1. Who is a Tasty Treat Express Delivery Partner? +
Tasty Treat Express Delivery Partners are independent contractors who pick up food or grocery orders from restaurants/outlets and deliver them to customers. They use their own vehicles for deliveries.
2. How to become a Tasty Treat Express Delivery Partner? +
Visit our website or app, navigate to the "Become a Delivery Partner" section, and follow the registration process. Provide necessary identification, vehicle details, and required documents.
3. What are the requirements to become a Tasty Treat Express Delivery Partner? +
You need a valid ID, a two-wheeler or bicycle, a smartphone, and flexible hours. A background check may be conducted.
4. How can I track my order? +
You can track your order in real-time through our app or website. Go to the "My Orders" section and click on the order to view its current status.
5. What should I do if my order is delayed? +
If your order is delayed, please check the tracking status for updates. You can also contact our support team through the app or website for assistance.
6. Can I cancel my order? +
Yes, you can cancel your order before it is prepared by the restaurant. Go to the "My Orders" section, select the order, and click on "Cancel Order." Cancellation policies may apply.
7. What payment methods are accepted? +
We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, digital wallets, and cash on delivery. You can select your preferred payment method at checkout.
8. How do I report an issue with my order? +
If you encounter an issue with your order, go to the "Help" section in the app or website and select the relevant order. You can report the issue and our support team will assist you.
9. Are there any delivery charges? +
Delivery charges may vary based on your location, the restaurant, and the order value. The charges will be displayed at checkout before you confirm your order.
10. Can I schedule an order for later? +
Yes, you can schedule an order for a later time. During checkout, select the "Schedule Order" option and choose your preferred delivery time.
